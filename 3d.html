Our channel "VR Zone:
Virtual Reality Technologies"

Channel description: "VR Zone" is a fascinating and informative channel
about the latest innovations in the world of virtual reality. We talk about the
most advanced VR headsets, devices and applications, conduct reviews, tests
and comparisons to help our viewers make the right choice when buying virtual
reality equipment. Our hosts Maxim and Kirill understand the technical details and
capabilities of new devices, and also share tips on using VR technologies in
everyday life. And presenters Alexey and Elizaveta conduct fascinating and
extraordinary tests of the technology, showing its capabilities in various situations,
from gaming scenarios to professional use.

The history of the VR glasses
store began back in 2015

The history of the VR glasses store began back in 2015, when virtual
reality technologies were just beginning to gain popularity. The store
also actively participated in charity events aimed at supporting children
and adults with disabilities, for example, the “VR for Everyone” campaign.
As part of the annual festivals, master classes, lectures from experts
in the field of virtual reality, as well as demonstrations of the latest
developments in this field were held.
